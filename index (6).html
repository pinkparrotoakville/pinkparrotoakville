Our Story

More than
just a treat.

Pink Parrot is Oakville's go-to superfood café for people who want more from their food. Rooted in the belief that healthy eating should feel indulgent, Pink Parrot redefines wellness with bold flavors, vibrant ingredients, and a creative twist.

We craft nourishing bowls, specialty beverages, and wholesome snacks using real, thoughtfully sourced ingredients designed to energize and satisfy.
